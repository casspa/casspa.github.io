BARBIE FASHION CLOSET

UI Designer | MIGHTY KINGDOM

Illustrator | Photoshop | Figma

Barbie Fashion Closet was one of the first projects I joined, after starting at Mighty Kingdom. I was tasked with creating mockups for the upcoming Barbie Pass feature. This involved working with the Producer and Product manager, as well as the projects 2D/UI artist, to create UI layout, assets and navigation

Some of my first memories of gaming are playing Barbie Horse Adventures (2003), so having the opportunity to work with Mattel was an exciting start to my career!

The assets were created in Illustrator, the panels in Photoshop and the navigation and any transitions/animation were done in Figma
