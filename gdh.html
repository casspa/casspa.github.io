GABBY’ DOLLHOUSE: GAMES & CATS

UI Designer | MIGHTY KINGDOM

Figma | Illustrator

Based on the Netflix series, Gabby’s Dollhouse is a game which promotes imagination and exploration, through puzzles, mini-games and interaction with Gabby and her kitten friends!

Mainly using Figma, I worked on multiple flows and features, such as the such as the Photobooth, Store, Construction Meta Game and Character Traversal

Initially, Gabby’s Dollhouse contained multiple rooms with only one cat companion in each of those rooms. To promote more interactivity with different characters, we created the option to bring any cat into any room

All screens and flows were created in Figma, and recorded GIFs of the prototype were used for internal and client approval, as well as helping the programmers to visualise what needed to be implemented


The Photobooth was another fun feature, that I got to be part of, which allowed players to use their camera to take photos with characters, stickers and props, which were then exported to their gallery

The last features I worked on, was the Meta Game, where players could edit their own section of each room, and add different items or furniture, as well as purchasing extra items from the Store, the Meta Game, where players could edit their own section of each room, and add different items or furniture, as well as purchasing extra items from the Store that we would also be creating

After completing some competitor analysis of other games that do this well, I created a wireframes and then high fidelity versions of these features.
