PETER RABBIT RUN!

UI Designer | MIGHTY KINGDOM

Figma | Trello

After the release of Peter Rabbit Run, I joined the team for a few weeks to see where we could make small changes, that would result in a better experience for players

I worked with the Product Manager, Game Designer and Marketing to identify and prototype any areas of improvement, which was done by testing the game, reading player feedback, competitor analysis, creating usability reviews and refining any suggestions through an effort to impact summary

Once we collated the data, from both the players and our own experiences, we identified confusion and areas of disconnect between the different types of task/challenges in the game and how this relates to their multiplier.

Some of the solutions included adding clear visual connections between completing a Run Challenge and the multiplier increasing and creating clearer distinction between the Daily Tasks vs the Run Challenges, both visible from the Pause Screen

We also wanted to improve retention for PVP races, by ensuring only races over 20 seconds were used, in the hopes this would promote a more positive and competitive experience for players

Panel mockups included Follow Us, FAQ, Store Costume/Currency Pack panels, and flows to share Leaderboard results with friends and the updated Leaderboard/Challenge flows, to help communicate progress to players
